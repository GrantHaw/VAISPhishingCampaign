How real attackers succeed

  • Urgency & Fear → “update your account in 10 min or it will be locked.”
  • Authority → “IT Support” or “Payroll” demanding you act now.
  • Curiosity/Reward → “See confidential results here.”

3 ways to spot a phish

  • Hover before you click—does the URL match the real domain?
  • Verify strange requests out-of-band (call the sender directly).
  • Watch for subtle misspellings and odd grammar.
